Side-Channel Analysis for Hardware Trojan Detection: A Deep Learning Approach
Molcer, P.S., Abdesselam, A.N. (2025)
In: Kovács, T.A., Fürstner, I. (eds) Critical Infrastructure Protection: Advanced Technologies for Crisis Prevention and Response. NATO ATC 2024. NATO Science for Peace and Security Series C: Environmental Security. Springer, Dordrecht.
This research presents a novel deep learning framework for detecting hardware Trojans in System-on-Chip (SoC) architectures through side-channel analysis. The approach leverages convolutional and feedforward neural networks to analyze power consumption patterns and electromagnetic emissions, enabling the identification of malicious hardware modifications that could compromise IoT device security. The methodology demonstrates significant improvements in detection accuracy compared to traditional statistical methods, contributing to enhanced security measures for embedded systems in critical infrastructure applications.
